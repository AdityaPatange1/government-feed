## Overview

Government Feed is an official app created by the BJ government to monitor the environment. With this app, the user sees how things change in the surrounding, and that allows the government agents to detect anomalies and trace whereabouts for legal purposes. By virtue of this app, you see the strong resemblence between what you are doing vs what is happening around you, and that helps you upgrade your agentic meditation practice. This app leverages text based feeds, and also provides a detailed screen to view all occurrences in the environment. You also get a report button, and several shortcut options to trigger 'actions' which are then reported to the BJ government, Bhavana Society Of West Virginia, Isha Foundation, and Sasaki Roshi Foundation Of Corporate Affairs.
